# Life Is a Project: The Story Behind the Framework

Twenty years of enterprise transformation work will teach you a few things about projects.

It will teach you that the most sophisticated project management tools in the world cannot save a project whose PM does not know what they are building. It will teach you that the highest-performing teams are not the ones with the best technology — they are the ones whose members know their roles, their constraints, and their purpose.

And if you pay close enough attention, it will teach you something that has nothing to do with corporate projects at all: that the same discipline separating a successful project from a failed one is exactly what separates a purposeful life from a reactive one.

That realization became Life Is a Project™.

## The Gap I Couldn't Stop Seeing

I spent years helping organizations build better project systems. Better charters. Better scope management. Better stakeholder engagement. And it worked — consistently, measurably.

But I kept noticing something. The same professionals who ran flawless sprints at work came home to vague goals, reactive days, and annual reviews they never wrote. The discipline that made them excellent at work never transferred to the most important project they would ever manage: themselves.

And if that gap existed for seasoned professionals, it was enormous for the young people entering the workforce.

I couldn't stop seeing it.

## Why Young Professionals

I could have built a program for executives. The market is there, the budget is there, and the need is real.

But the executives already have coaches, mentors, and fifteen productivity systems they've tried and abandoned. Young professionals lack both the tools and the framework. And the earlier you give someone a proven system for executing their life intentionally, the more of their life they get to live with that advantage.

The 7 Project Principles™ exist because I believe every 18-year-old deserves the same professional discipline that took me twenty years to earn.

## Faith and Framework

I cannot separate my faith from my work. Life Is a Project™ is not a secular program wearing faith language. It is a program built on the conviction that your life is a God-given project, and you are called to manage it with excellence.

For contexts where that framing fits, we offer the Kingdom Edition — integrating the framework with the 7 Spheres of Influence. For secular contexts, the Leadership Edition delivers the same professional methodology without theological framing.

Both are built on the same foundation: your life is worth managing with the seriousness of a project that matters.

## What's Next

Wiser Generations is the platform. Life Is a Project™ is the first program. The community, the blog, the certification pathway — all of it exists to serve one purpose: equipping the next generation to manage their most important projects with excellence.
